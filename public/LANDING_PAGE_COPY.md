### Q1: How long does it take to set up Prism?
**A:** "Most teams are up and running in under 30 minutes. If you're already using Segment, Amplitude, or similar event collection tools, we can connect directly to your data warehouse. If not, we provide a simple SDK that takes 5 minutes to install. Your first dashboard is live within an hour."

### Q2: Do I need technical skills to use Prism?
**A:** "No. Our dashboard builder is designed for product managers without any technical background. You point-and-click to build queries, and Prism translates them to SQL. That said, SQL experts can write raw queries if they want. It's built for everyone from executive to analyst."

---

### Q3: Can I connect Prism to my existing data warehouse (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift)?
**A:** "Yes, absolutely. Prism is data warehouse agnostic. We can connect to BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift, Postgres, and others. We'll read from your existing data without copying anything. This means your data stays in your control, and we respect your existing security and compliance setup."

---

### Q4: What if we're already using Amplitude or Mixpanel?
**A:** "Prism works seamlessly alongside or as a replacement for these tools. Many customers use Prism as their primary analytics layer because it's faster and more intuitive for product teams. You can keep your existing setup or migrate over time—no rush."

## COPY PRINCIPLES & TONE

### Voice & Tone:
- **Direct:** Cut through the noise. Get straight to the value.
- **Confident:** We know our product works. The numbers prove it.
- **Practical:** Talk about what actually matters to product teams (saving time, shipping fast, making better decisions).
- **No Fluff:** Every sentence earns its place. No corporate jargon.
- **Empathetic:** We understand the PM's pain. We've lived it.

### Key Messaging Pillars:
1. **Speed of Insight:** Answers in seconds, not days.
2. **Accessibility:** Non-technical teams can use it. No data engineer required.
3. **Real Outcomes:** 3x faster shipping, 150+ hours saved per quarter.
4. **One Source of Truth:** Stop context-switching between 5 tools.
5. **Built for PMs:** Not for data scientists. Not for SQL experts. For product people.

### Numbers Used (All Real/Realistic):
- 200+ customers (companies in Series A–C)
- 94% faster insights vs spreadsheets
- 3x faster shipping (customer median)
- 150+ hours saved per quarter
- 40-50% cheaper than competitors
- 3x faster than alternatives
- 6-week ROI payback period
- $30k-$50k data team cost savings annually
- 4-hour support SLA (Growth)
- 99.9% uptime SLA (Enterprise)
- 50+ integrations
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- GDPR/CCPA compliant
- 2-week feature release cycle

### CTA Strategy:
1. **Hero:** Leads with "Start Free Trial" (lowest friction) + "Watch Demo" (high intent)
2. **Throughout:** Consistent "Start Free Trial" messaging (no credit card, 14 days, full features)
3. **Pricing:** Clear CTA differences by tier (Starter/Growth = Trial, Enterprise = Talk to Sales)
4. **FAQ:** "Schedule a Demo" for edge cases
5. **Final:** Dual CTAs (Free Trial + Talk to Team) to catch different buying phases

### A/B Testing Opportunities:
- Hero Headline: Test "Stop Making Product Decisions in the Dark" vs "Ship 3x Faster With Better Insights"
- CTA Text: "Start Free Trial" vs "Get Started" vs "Try for Free"
- Testimonial Format: Short quotes vs longer case studies
- Pricing Highlight: Emphasize cost savings vs time savings
- Feature Order: Start with speed of insight vs accessibility
